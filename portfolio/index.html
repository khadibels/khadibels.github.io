About Me

I am Nicole, a 4th-year IT student with a strong passion for software engineering, web development, and mobile development. My academic journey and projects have allowed me to gain hands-on experience in full-stack web development, PHP/MySQL, database design, and front-end technologies, as well as beginner-level exposure to Flutter and Dart for mobile applications.

I am particularly interested in creating systems that are functional, efficient, and user-friendly, with a focus on solving real-world problems. My work includes building modular web-based applications that integrate features such as inventory tracking, procurement, logistics, and document management, all while ensuring consistent UI/UX and scalability.

I value continuous learning, adaptability, and collaboration, and I aim to further enhance my skills to pursue a professional career as a software engineer or web/mobile developer.
